## Flight Research: Penang (PEN) ↔ Tbilisi (TBS)

Jack requested cheapest and shortest travel options from Penang to Tbilisi and back, July-September 2026 timeframe.

### Key Findings
- **No direct flights** exist for PEN↔TBS; all routes require transit
- **Cheapest round-trip (7-day trip):** MYR 2,878 via Google Flights calendar view
  - Best dates: Sundays and mid-week (July 5, 7, 9, 11, 12, 14, 16, 18, 19, 21, 23, 26, 28, 30)
  - Avoid Mondays (MYR 5,000+)
- **Return leg (TBS→PEN, Sep 1→Sep 7):** MYR 3,267 (flydubai via Dubai, 1 stop, 19h)
- **Total round-trip estimate:** MYR 6,000–7,000
- **Shortest travel time:** ~18h 45m (Qatar Airways via Doha)
- **Best value airline:** flydubai (1 stop Dubai, reasonable price & duration)

### Research Challenges
- Google Flights was the only viable source; other aggregators (Skyscanner, Kayak, Kiwi, Expedia) all blocked by CAPTCHAs/bot detection
- Browser automation used (Playwright) to navigate Google Flights
- Calendar price view worked well; detailed itinerary extraction was partial
- Multiple browser timeouts encountered; gateway restarts needed
