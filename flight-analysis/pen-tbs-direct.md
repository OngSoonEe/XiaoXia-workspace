# PEN↔TBS Direct Flight Analysis (End-Sep to Nov 2026)
## Criteria: Shortest travel time, 3-4 full weekday days in Tbilisi, cheapest

## Methodology
1. Identify shortest route PEN↔TBS → 1-stop via Dubai (Flydubai/Emirates codeshare)
2. Scan all valid Mon-Thu depart, Thu-Fri return date combos (to get 3-4 weekday stays)
3. Filter: arrival TBS day N, departure TBS day N+3 or N+4, with ≥3 full weekdays
4. Rank by: shortest time first, then cheapest price

## Key Findings

### Cheapest Options (MYR 3,239 RT, Emirates Operated by Flydubai)
**5 date combos at this price:**
1. Oct 19 Mon → Oct 23 Fri (3 weekdays ✅)
2. Nov 2 Mon → Nov 6 Fri (3 weekdays ✅)
3. Nov 9 Mon → Nov 13 Fri (3 weekdays ✅)
4. Nov 23 Mon → Nov 27 Fri (3 weekdays ✅)
5. Oct 19 Mon → Oct 26 Mon (4+ weekdays ✅)

### Flight Details (same for all MYR 3,239 options)
- **Outbound**: PEN 21:35 → DXB → TBS 11:35+1 (7h30m, 1-stop)
- **Return**: TBS → DXB → PEN (via Flydubai, ~8-12h)
- **Aircraft**: Flydubai Boeing 737 MAX 8 / 737-800
- **Booked as**: Emirates flight number (codeshare)

### Price Pattern
- Emirates codeshare available on: ~every other Mon (odd weeks in Oct, every Mon in Nov)
- Emirates codeshare NOT available: Oct 12, Oct 26 (even weeks?)
- Pure Flydubai is 33-50% MORE expensive (MYR 4,291-4,907)
- Nov is consistently cheaper than early Oct
- Price does NOT change with stay duration (3-day vs 4-day same price)

### Why Emirates Codeshare is Cheaper
Emirates sells Flydubai-operated flights under their code at lower fares as part of the Emirates-Flydubai partnership. Same plane, same crew, lower price.
