### Context
Jack asked me to analyze Intel JaguarShore (JGS) use-case recipe (UR) and cross-check with quantization recipe (QR) docx. 5-step analysis: validate UR, identify quantization needs, cross-check with QR, compile gap report, zip and email.

### Key Learnings
- **JGS vs CRI quantization formats:**
  - CRI (Gaudi): MXFP8 (8-bit), MXFP4 (4-bit, E8M0 scale, block 32)
  - JGS (next-gen): MXFP8 (8-bit), NVFP4 (4-bit, UE4M3 scale, block 16 + FP32 global scale)
  - MXFP4 is NOT the JGS 4-bit format — NVFP4 is
  - DeepSeek R1 has BOTH MXFP4 (CRI, AutoRound) and NVFP4 (JGS, RTN) recipes in QR
  - MXFP8 is shared across both targets

- **UR has 8 use-cases:**
  1. Dense 70B SFT+PO (Llama-3.1-70B)
  2. Flux.1 Schnell Image Gen (12B)
  3. RAFT Retrieval-Aware FT (Dense 30-70B)
  4. VLM SFT (Llama4 Maverick, MoE ~17B active)
  5. MoE Expert Expansion (Llama4 Maverick-style)
  6. Agentic RL (DeepSeek-V3, 671B MoE, 37B active)
  7. Large→Small Distillation (70B→7-14B)
  8. Code + Safety Alignment (DeepSeek-Coder-V2 / DeepSeek-R1)

- **Critical gaps identified:**
  - UC6 (DeepSeek-V3): No quantization recipe at all — DeepSeek-V3 ≠ DeepSeek-R1 (different model)
  - UC4/5 (Llama4 Maverick): NVFP4 WIP, FP8 KV/Attn unvalidated
  - UC8 (DeepSeek-Coder-V2): No recipe exists
  - UC2 (Flux): NVFP4 not started (but MXFP8 exists; may not need 4-bit for Schnell)
  - DLRM: Both MXFP8 and NVFP4 WIP

- **Initial analysis error:** I incorrectly stated "JGS doesn't support MXFP4" as a blanket claim. Corrected after Jack challenged it — the QR does include MXFP4 for CRI, and DeepSeek R1 has both MXFP4 (CRI) and NVFP4 (JGS) recipes.
